About Me

Dan is a Director of Photography located in New York. He also has been with J-Cut Films, Inc. for a couple years, editing a wide variety of projects. He has advanced technical skills in not only editing, but also as a videographer and photographer. His creative career began while filming friends snowboarding, and from that his pursuit has been to become better in all aspects of being a video professional. He has experience directing and producing not only for J-Cut, but side projects such as music videos, live music recordings, and other personal projects. Dan has experience collaborating with companies like VMware, Dell, Microsoft, Neurothink, Collibra, Google, and Burton Snowboards.
